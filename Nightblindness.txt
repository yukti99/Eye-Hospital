Nightblindness

Symptoms:
lack of sight during night.Night blindness caused by nearsightedness, cataracts, or vitamin A deficiency 
trouble seeing in dim light even with corrective lenses.

Treatment:
Curable deficiency disease
Corrective lenses, such as eyeglasses or contacts,
can improve nearsighted vision both during the day and at night.


Advice:
Go for detailed medical examination and blood test to measure Vitamin A deficiency and cause of night blindness.
Increase Vitamin A intake according to your specialist's prescription.
